## Our Commitment to Privacy

Expense Split is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

## Information We Collect

### Analytics Data
We use privacy-focused analytics to improve the app. This service:
- Collects anonymous usage data
- Does not track personal information
- Does not use cookies or persistent identifiers
- Complies with GDPR and privacy regulations

### Purchase Information
We use RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases. RevenueCat:
- Processes App Store purchases
- Manages premium feature access
- Does not have access to your payment information
- Stores only necessary transaction data

## Data Storage

### Your Expense Data
- All your groups, expenses, and financial data are stored locally on your device
- If enabled, data is synced through iCloud using your personal Apple ID
- We cannot access your personal content

### CloudKit Sharing
When you share a group with others:
- Data is shared directly between devices using Apple's CloudKit
- Shared data is encrypted in transit
- We do not have access to shared group data
- Only invited participants can access shared groups

### CSV Export
- Exports are generated locally on your device
- No export data is transmitted to our servers
- You control where exports are saved

## Data We Don't Collect
- Personal identification information
- Financial account details or payment methods
- Location data
- Contact information beyond group participant names you manually enter
- Third-party analytics or tracking

## Third-Party Services

### Apple Services
The app uses Apple's built-in services:
- iCloud for optional data sync (encrypted)
- CloudKit for group sharing (encrypted)
- App Store for purchases (handled by Apple)

### No Account Required
- No registration or login needed
- No email addresses collected
- No passwords stored
- Start using the app immediately

## Your Rights
You have the right to:
- Delete all your data by uninstalling the app
- Disable iCloud sync at any time
- Stop sharing groups whenever you want
- Export your data as CSV files

## Data Security
- All data is stored locally on your device
- iCloud sync uses Apple's encryption
- CloudKit sharing is encrypted in transit
- No data stored on third-party servers

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
