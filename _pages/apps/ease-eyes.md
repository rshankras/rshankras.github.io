## Protect Your Eyes, One Break at a Time

Long hours at the screen can take a toll on your eyes. Ease Eyes helps you maintain healthy eye habits through smart break reminders based on the proven **20-20-20 rule**: every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds.

## Why Your Eyes Need This

### The Problem
- **Digital Eye Strain** affects over 50% of computer users
- Extended screen time causes dry eyes, headaches, and blurred vision
- Most people forget to take breaks when focused on work
- Traditional timers disrupt workflow and are easy to ignore

### The Solution
Ease Eyes runs quietly in your menu bar, intelligently reminding you to rest your eyes without disrupting your flow. It pauses during meetings, tracks your screen time, and helps you build sustainable eye care habits.

## Features

### 20-20-20 Rule Made Easy
- Customizable break intervals (10-120 minutes)
- Quick presets: 20, 25, or 30-minute cycles
- Short breaks use gentle notifications
- Long breaks offer optional full-screen reminders
- Follow the scientifically-backed 20-20-20 rule

### Smart Break Management
- **Short Breaks**: Gentle notifications every 20 minutes—minimal disruption
- **Long Breaks**: Choose notification or full-screen window every 60 minutes

## The Science Behind It

The **20-20-20 rule** is recommended by ophthalmologists worldwide:

> Every **20 minutes**, look at something **20 feet away** for at least **20 seconds**.

This simple practice helps:
- Reduce eye muscle fatigue
- Encourage natural blinking
- Prevent dry eyes
- Lower risk of digital eye strain
- Maintain long-term eye health
