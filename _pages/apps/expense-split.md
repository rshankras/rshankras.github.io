### Key Screens Overview

**Group List**
See all your expense groups at a glance. Each group shows member count and total expenses, so you always know what's inside before opening.

**Smart Categories**
Type "Uber" and the app suggests "Travel". One tap and done. AI-powered categorization learns as you use the app.

**Custom Splits with Lock**
Lock anyone's share and it stays fixed while you adjust everyone else. Perfect for complex splits where some amounts need to stay the same.

**Quick Add Users**
The app suggests people from your other groups. No need to retype names or search through contacts.

**Settle Up**
See exactly who owes whom with crystal-clear balance breakdowns. The app simplifies debts to the fewest transactions needed.

**Share Groups**
Invite friends and roommates via CloudKit. Set permissions for view-only or editing access. Everyone stays in sync automatically.
