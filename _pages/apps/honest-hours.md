## Why You Need This

### The Problem
- You finish work feeling busy but unsure what you actually accomplished
- Social media and news sites steal your focus without you realizing
- Traditional time tracking requires manual input nobody maintains
- Most productivity apps send your data to the cloud

### The Solution
Honest Hours runs quietly in your menu bar, automatically tracking where your time goes. When you need to focus, it blocks distracting apps and websites. All data stays on your Mac—no accounts, no cloud, no tracking.

## Features

### Automatic Time Tracking
- Monitors active apps and websites every 5 seconds
- Tracks Safari, Chrome, and Brave browser tabs
- Shows visit frequency (how many times you switched to each app/site)
- Idle detection pauses tracking after 5 minutes of inactivity
- No manual input required—just work naturally

### Focus Mode with App Blocking
- Block distracting apps and websites during focus sessions
- Quick-start 25 or 60 minute sessions (Pomodoro-friendly)
- Automatic switch-away when you try to open blocked apps
- Track block attempts and abandoned sessions
- Smart insights based on your focus patterns

### Dashboard & Insights
- Today's total time tracked at a glance
- Top activities with visit counts and duration
- Date range picker: Today, Yesterday, This Week
- "View All" with search for complete activity list
- Understand your patterns to work smarter

### Menu Bar Quick Access
- See your current activity and time tracked
- Top 3 activities always visible
- Start focus sessions with one click
- Pause tracking when needed
- Minimal footprint—never clutters your workspace

### Smart Focus Insights
- Track completed vs abandoned sessions
- See how many distractions were blocked
- Get personalized suggestions based on your patterns
- Recent sessions show actual vs planned duration
- Learn what works for your focus style

## What Gets Tracked

**Tracked:**
- Active application name
- Browser tab titles and URLs (Safari, Chrome, Brave)
- Time spent per app/page
- Visit frequency (context switches)

**NOT Tracked:**
- Keyboard input
- Screenshots
- File contents
- Network traffic
- Anything sent to external servers

## 100% Private by Design

- **All Local**: Data stored on your Mac using CoreData
- **No Cloud**: Zero network calls or external servers
- **No Analytics**: No telemetry or usage tracking
- **No Account**: Start immediately, no sign-up required
- **Your Data**: Export anytime, delete everything with one click

## Technical Excellence

- Built with SwiftUI for a native macOS experience
- MVVM architecture for reliability
- CoreData for efficient local storage
- NSWorkspace + AppleScript for accurate tracking
- Optimized for macOS 12.4+ (Monterey and later)
- Universal binary (Intel and Apple Silicon)
